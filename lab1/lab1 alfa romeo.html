History: Alfa Romeo was founded in Milan, Italy, in 1910. The company's name is a combination of "Anonima Lombarda Fabbrica Automobili" (Anonymous Lombard Automobile Factory). Motorsports Success: Alfa Romeo has a long history in motorsports, including Formula 1 racing.
