Benefits of using flexbox:

Simplified Layout: Flexbox simplifies complex layouts with less code.
Responsive Design: It's inherently responsive, adapting to different screens.
Efficient Space Distribution: Easily distribute space among items.
Alignment Control: Fine-grained control over item alignment.
No Floats or Positioning Hacks: Eliminates complex layout hacks.
Ordering: Change visual order without altering source order.
Single-Dimensional Layout: Ideal for rows or columns within grids.
Auto-Sizing: Items can size based on content or available space.
No Fixed Widths: Allows flexible element sizing.
Browser Compatibility: Well-supported in modern browsers.
Intuitive Syntax: Easy-to-understand syntax.
Community Adoption: Widely used with abundant resources.
